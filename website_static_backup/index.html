    FAQ

    Questions? We've got answers.

    What is ReplyPals?

    ReplyPals is a Chrome extension that rewrites your text to sound completely natural to native English speakers. It's built specifically for people who learned English as a second language and want to communicate more confidently.

    Which websites does it work on?

    ReplyPals works on every website where you can type — Gmail, WhatsApp Web, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Slack, Notion, Outlook, and any other website with a text field.

    What's the Native Sound Score?

    After every rewrite, ReplyPals gives your text a score from 0-100 based on how natural it sounds to a native English speaker. 90+ means it's indistinguishable from a native writer. You can track your improvement over time.

    Does ReplyPals store my emails?

    No. Your text is sent to our AI for rewriting and immediately deleted. We never store, read, or share your writing. See our Privacy Policy.

    What languages does it support?

    You can write in Hindi, Arabic, Filipino (Tagalog), Malayalam, Portuguese, Spanish, or French and ReplyPals will translate and rewrite in natural English automatically.

    How is this different from Grammarly?

    Grammarly fixes grammar. ReplyPals fixes tone and non-native patterns. If you write grammatically correct but unnatural English (like "please do the needful"), Grammarly won't catch it. ReplyPals will.

    Can I use it for WhatsApp messages?

    Yes. ReplyPals works inside WhatsApp Web. A small button appears in your message box — click it and your message is rewritten before you send.

    Can I use my voice instead of typing?

    Yes! Click the microphone button and speak in English, Malayalam, Hindi, Arabic, or any supported language. ReplyPals transcribes and rewrites your speech automatically. No typing needed.

    What happens when I hit the free limit?

    On the free plan you get 10 rewrites per month. After that, you can upgrade to Starter or Pro. Pricing is automatically adjusted for your country.
