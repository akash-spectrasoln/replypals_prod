Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

ReplyPals helps users rewrite and generate text. We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling data responsibly.

What we collect

  • Account information (email, authentication metadata).
  • Usage metadata (feature used, timestamps, model/provider, status, scores).
  • Billing information via Stripe (managed by Stripe).

What we do not store

  • We do not permanently store the original user writing text for analytics purposes.
  • We do not sell personal data.

How data is used

  • To provide rewrite/generate functionality.
  • To enforce usage limits and subscription entitlements.
  • To improve reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

Third-party processors

  • Supabase (authentication/database)
  • Stripe (payments)
  • AI providers (for model inference)

Retention

Operational metadata is retained as needed for billing, security, and support. You can request account deletion by contacting support.

Contact

For privacy requests, email support@replypals.in or use our contact form.
